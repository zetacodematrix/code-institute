Winter Loop – Erith to Banstead Downs (Sections 1–6)

Cool, crisp days along the south London reaches of the LOOP, from the Thames marshes at Erith to the chalk downs at Banstead.

Distances and section names follow the official London Outer Orbital Path (London LOOP). These notes give our broad plan for the season’s Sunday walks.

Section 1 - Erith to Old Bexley

Approx. 8.5 miles · official London LOOP section

Follows the signed LOOP between Erith and Old Bexley, using a mix of riverside paths, parks and suburban links. Terrain is typical outer London: mostly easy gradients with occasional muddy or uneven stretches.

We confirm meeting times, café and transport notes by email in the week before the walk.
