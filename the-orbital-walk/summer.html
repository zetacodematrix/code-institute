Summer Loop - Harefield West to Chingford (Sections 13-18)

Longer days for woodland and parkland around Harefield, Moor Park and Enfield, finishing on the edge of Epping Forest at Chingford.

Distances and section names follow the official London Outer Orbital Path (London LOOP). These notes give our broad plan for the season’s Sunday walks.

Section 13 – Harefield West to Moor Park

Approx. 5.0 miles · official London LOOP section

Follows the signed LOOP between Harefield West and Moor Park, using a mix of riverside paths, parks and suburban links. Terrain is typical outer London: mostly easy gradients with occasional muddy or uneven stretches.

We confirm meeting times, café and transport notes by email in the week before the walk.
