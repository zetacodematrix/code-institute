Spring Loop - Banstead Downs to Harefield West (Sections 7–12)

Gentler north-west London countryside as the path swings via Kingston, the River Crane and the Grand Union Canal to Harefield.

Distances and section names follow the official London Outer Orbital Path (London LOOP). These notes give our broad plan for the seaso'’s Sunday walks.

Section 7 - Banstead Downs to Ewell

Approx. 3.5 miles · official London LOOP section

Follows the signed LOOP between Banstead Downs and Ewell, using a mix of riverside paths, parks and suburban links. Terrain is typical outer London: mostly easy gradients with occasional muddy or uneven stretches.

We confirm meeting times, café and transport notes by email in the week before the walk.
