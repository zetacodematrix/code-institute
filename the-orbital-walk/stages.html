The Orbital route stages

The Orbital follows a broad loop roughly tracking the M25, but favouring footpaths, commons and green corridors. Below are the main stages we walk on Sundays throughout 2026.

Walks & Route Stages

The Orbital follows the official London Outer Orbital Path (London LOOP) – nearly 150 miles of waymarked path around Greater London. We've grouped the 24 LOOP sections into four seasonal batches. Click a season to see the stages we aim to walk on Sundays in that part of the year.

Winter Loop

Sections 1-6 · Erith → Banstead Downs · ~45 miles total

Frosty Thames estuary, River Cray and open downland – great for cool, clear days and earlier finishes.

View winter stages →
Spring Loop

Sections 7-12 · Banstead Downs → Harefield West · ~35 miles total

Rolling suburbs, lavender fields and canal‑side paths as the LOOP swings north-west through Kingston and Uxbridge.

View spring stages →
Summer Loop

Sections 13–18 · Harefield West → Chingford · ~42 miles total

Longer woodland and parkland days with big views over outer London, ideal for the longest daylight.

View summer stages →
Autumn Loop

Sections 19-24 · Chingford → Purfleet · ~28 miles total

North-east London woods, windmills and marshes, finishing beside the Thames at Purfleet.

View autumn stages →
