Walk the London Orbital, one café at a time.

The Orbital is a friendly community project to walk the full M25 circle in relaxed Sunday stages - starting near a café and ending at a cosy pub or rest stop.

Join a Sunday walk Explore the stages

A year-long challenge, at a human pace.

Across 2026, we'll complete the London M25 Walking Circle in carefully planned stages. Each route is between 8 and 14 miles, with plenty of time for coffee stops, photos, and chatting with fellow walkers.

Whether you join us for one section or commit to the full orbital, you'll discover green corridors, rivers, historic villages, and unexpected views of London's skyline.

  • Guided Sunday walks throughout 2026
  • Café meeting points and end-of-walk pub stops
  • Mixture of towpaths, woodland, commons and urban edges
